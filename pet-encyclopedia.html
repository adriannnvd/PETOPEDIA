German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is an intelligent, loyal, and versatile dog breed originating from Germany. Known for their strength, agility, and protective nature, they excel as working dogs in roles like herding, police work, and search and rescue. Their affectionate and devoted temperament makes them great family companions.
